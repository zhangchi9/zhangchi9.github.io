Chi Zhang

Chi Zhang, Ph.D.

Data Scientist | Ph.D. of Physics | Kaggle competitions expert

I completed my doctoral studies under the supervision of Prof. Armen Stepanyants at the Neurogeometry lab in the Department of Physics at Northeastern University. I got my bachelor's degree from Lanzhou University in China. My research is mainly focused on using statistical physics, mathematical and machine learning methods to understand the dynamics and the function of the brain. Currently, I am developing algorithms to reconstruct C.elegans cellbodies and dendrites. More detail about my research can be found in my research page.
